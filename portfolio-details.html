Residential Rooftop Solar System

This 3kW residential installation features premium monocrystalline solar panels with an efficiency rating of 21%. The system includes a state-of-the-art inverter and comprehensive monitoring system that allows the homeowner to track energy production in real-time through a convenient mobile app.

The installation was completed in just two days with minimal disruption to the homeowner's daily routine. The system is expected to offset approximately 90% of the household's electricity consumption, resulting in significant monthly savings on utility bills and a complete return on investment within 5-6 years.
